Best Time To Visit Taj Mahal In 2026

06 May 2026 Admin

The Taj Mahal is one of the most beautiful monuments in the world and attracts millions of visitors every year. The best time to visit is during sunrise when the marble glows beautifully under soft golden light.

Winter season between October and March offers pleasant weather for sightseeing and photography. Avoid peak afternoon hours during summer because temperatures can be extremely high.

Book tickets in advance and prefer weekday visits for a smoother travel experience.
